Image Standards

You can upload images of any size or aspect ratio up to 2048x2048, but you should optimally upload images with minimum resolution of 720x720.

We resize your images according to the table shown below. Images with an accurate resolution look more elegant and professional.

When you upload an image of a product, LimeSpot automatically creates different image sizes of it that are used in different scenarios. For example, a product image’s Large version may be used in the product details page, where your customer views a specific product (with mouse over zoom functionality). A smaller version of the product image will be displayed on your product catalog, which shows many products at once.

Also remember to keep a consistent aspect ratio (the ratio of an image's width to its height) for all your products images as this maintains a uniform user experience.

While adding images for banners or other marketing graphics, please ensure that you maintain an accurate aspect ratio (width by height) so that images fit in your store’s visible area. While resizing images we never alter the aspect ratio.

We never scale up original images. Thus if you upload a tiny image, it will remain tiny even if medium or large images are required. However, we scale down large images when necessary to improve the user experience.
