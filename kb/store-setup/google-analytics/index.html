Google Analytics

Google Analytics is a free service from Google that captures detailed data about a webpage's traffic and sale activity. It can be used to track your website's visitors from a variety of referral sources, including search engines, social networks, direct visits, or other websites. Your site's sales performance, transactions, revenue, and many other commerce metrics can be measured through its e-commerce features.

LimeSpot give you the option of integrating Google Analytics services with your LimeSpot-powered store. You need to have an operational Google Analytics account to do so.

If you do not have an existing Google analytics account, click here to create one and sign in using your Google account.

For further help on Google Analytics click here.

Set Up Google Analytics for your LimeSpot-Powered Store

  1. Log in to your Google Analytics account. You should see the following screen.
  2. Click on the Admin label present in the top ribbon. You should see the following screen.
  3. Click on the dropdown menu under Property and select 'Create a New Property'. You should see the following screen.
  4. Make sure that you are tracking a website and not a mobile app, then select a tracking method of your choice.
  5. Enter a name for your LimeSpot store.
  6. Enter the URL of your LimeSpot store. Usually it is in the format of www.limespot.com/xxxxx
  7. Choose your Industry and Time Zone.
  8. Click on Get Tracking ID and take note of the tracking ID.

The above steps ensure that you have successfully created an entry for your LimeSpot-powered store on your Google Analytics page. Now, you have to provide LimeSpot's engine with the tracking ID to complete the integration process.

  1. Log in to your LimeSpot Administrative Panel.
  2. Click on Analytics Setup under Store Settings.
  3. Enter your Tracking ID in the space provided.
  4. Enter www.limespot.com for Google Analytics Domain.
  5. Click Save to complete the process.
