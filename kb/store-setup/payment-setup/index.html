Using Payment Gateways

If you already have an e-commerce store LimeSpot can bring you enhanced personalization and customization while retaining your store's core infrastructure. As a result you can continue using your current payment gateways and processes. When a shopper purchases a product on a LimeSpot powered shop our system redirects user to your existing payment page which is automatically populated with required product details and payment amount. This makes the shopping experience easy and secure.

For small and medium businesses without any ecommerce presence, a fully functional LimeSpot store can be created in just a couple of hours. You can manually add products or use spreadsheets to upload multiple products. Currently, we accept PayPal as a preferred payment gateway partner for processing financial transactions. You should have a valid merchant account with PayPal and associate it with your LimeSpot powered online storefront. To set up a PayPal merchant account click here.

After creating a PayPal account, you should link it with your LimeSpot shop. You can click on Payment Gateways link from the Admin Panel or click here to connect your LimeSpot account with PayPal.

Select a desired PayPal Mode and enter user-credentials to validate your account. Our system supports two types of PayPal accounts.

  1. PayPal Standard Account. To create a standard PayPal account click here. It has no monthly fees and you can accept payments in 22 currencies from 193 countries.

    Enter your Merchant ID or Email address and click Save.

  2. PayPal Pay Flow Account. You can create an advanced PayPal account to get more control over your payment page and greater design flexibility. The monthly charges are $10 for PayPal Advanced and $35 for PayPal Pro plans. Click here to see the complete comparison chart for different PayPal accounts.

    Enter your Merchant ID, Username and Password to validate your PayPal account and click Save.
