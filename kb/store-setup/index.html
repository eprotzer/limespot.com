Store Setup

Congratulations on signing up for LimeSpot! You are on your way to creating a social, personalized experience for your customers.

Some of the next steps you should take in setting up your LimeSpot store include:

  • Setting up a Payment Gateway for your store
  • Setting up Google Analytics for your store so you can get detailed analysis on your store's performance

Once these are complete, we suggest setting up your store's products. Don't worry: this is very easy to do, and if you already have an online store with a major e-commerce provider it's automatic!
