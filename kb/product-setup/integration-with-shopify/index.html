Integration with Shopify

Importing products and collections from your Shopify store onto LimeSpot is a simple two-step process. Make sure that you have access to user credentials for Shopify store before you begin the integration process.

Below is an easy guide to synchronize LimeSpot with your Shopify store.

Set Up a Connection

  1. From the LimeSpot Admin Panel select Store Settings and then E-Commerce Connect.
  2. Enter your Shopify Store's name in the space provided and click Authorize.
  3. You will be redirected to the Shopify website. Enter your Shopify credentials to continue.
  4. You will be prompted to install the LimeSpot app for your Shopify store. Click on Install App to complete the process.

You will be redirected to the LimeSpot website and a message indicating successful authentication will be displayed.

Import your Products

  1. Click on Product Data Import from the Admin Panel.
  2. Select the button next to your Shopify store's name.
  3. Click on Import Products in the top right corner of the screen.

All your products and collections are now imported to LimeSpot platform and you can view these products under the Product section.

Please note that you should click on Import Products to synchronize updates every time you add more products or change product details on your Shopify store.
