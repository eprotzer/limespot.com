Integration with Listed E-Commerce Providers

LimeSpot gives you the ability for seamlessly integration of all your existing e-commerce infrastructure with LimeSpot platform. This means that if you already own and operate an online shop with any large ecommerce provider*, you can import and synchronize all your products and collections onto your LimeSpot powered shop in just a couple of clicks.

Through automatic integration, you can set up a fully functional personalized and social ecommerce storefront with LimeSpot in minutes.

*Currently we offer automatic integration support for following ecommerce providers.

  1. Shopify
  2. Magento
  3. BigCommerce

We are in the continuous process of adding more ecommerce providers to the list above. Check back for updates.

If you use another ecommerce provider, then please contact our customer desk and we will try to help with a custom plan for your business needs.

Follow the two steps mentioned below for automatic integration of your existing ecommerce store with LimeSpot platform.

  1. From the Admin Panel, click on Store Settings and then E-Commerce Connect.

    Enter the relevant details for your ecommerce store and click to validate the credentials. For specific help visit links below:

    1. Connect with Shopify
    2. Connect with Magento
    3. Connect with BigCommerce

  2. From the Admin Panel, click on Products and Contents and then Product Data Import.

    You will see the list of all validates ecommerce accounts on this page. Click on the desired account and hit Import Products button to import all your products and collections onto LimeSpot platform.

    Please note that you should click on Import Products to synchronize updates every time you add more products or change product details on your ecommerce store.

    Tip: You can see the imported products by clicking on Products link present under the section Products and Contents. The column titles as Source indicates the actual source of individual products.
