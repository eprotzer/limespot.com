What to Expect from your LimeSpot-Powered Store



LimeSpot-powered stores have a variety of exciting features that the world hasn't seen before. Here are just some which make LimeSpot the leader of the pack.

Advanced and Secure Personalization

Social networks are reputed as an incredibly rich, but as yet untapped, source of consumer information. When a shopper enters a LimeSpot powered store they're asked to authorize LimeSpot to enjoy a secure, personalized shopping experience. Once authorized, LimeSpot processes the information from the shopper's social network profile (e.g., Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn) to determine their demographic and tastes. LimeSpot then selects and shows the shopper specific products from your catalogues that they are likely to be interested in. Result - shoppers visiting your LimeSpot-powered store will immediately see the products they are most likely to purchase.

Furthermore, your shoppers will be confident knowing their social network information is safe as LimeSpot's engine operates without storing or sharing any profile data. It is real-time, on the fly personalization, based on who the shopper is now and not who they were.

Gift Giving

The great thing about the LimeSpot personalization engine is that it can work for your shoppers when they're in a gift-giving pinch. If a shopper goes to your LimeSpot-powered store in search of a gift for a loved one, they merely need to click on their friend's social profile to see what you would recommend.

Social Features

People love to shop together socially in real life. We think they should be able to shop together online too. Products in LimeSpot-powered stores can be rated, commented on, reviewed, or shared on a social network. Customers can use LimeSpot to interact directly with a brand or make group purchases.

A New Sales Channel

You can have LimeSpot-powered e-commerce on your traditional website and your Facebook page. The public will be able to browse your engaging social media posts and buy your products at the same time.
